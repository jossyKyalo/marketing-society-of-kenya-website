Who We Are

The Marketing Society of Kenya (MSK) is the premier umbrella body for marketing professionals in Kenya. Established in 1977, MSK champions excellence in marketing through training, certification, networking, and recognition of outstanding achievements in the field.

We are committed to empowering our members with the knowledge, tools, and networks they need to thrive in the evolving marketing landscape. Our members include professionals from agencies, corporates, SMEs, academia, and government institutions.

Our Mission

To champion, empower, and elevate the practice and profession of marketing in Kenya by providing strategic leadership, advocacy, and development opportunities to marketers across sectors.

  • Promote ethical marketing standards and practices.
  • Support professional development through training and certification.
  • Provide networking platforms for knowledge sharing and collaboration.

Our Vision

To be the leading marketing society in Africa, shaping the future of marketing through innovation, education, and impactful partnerships.

  • Inspire marketing leadership across all sectors.
  • Advance marketing knowledge and research.
  • Celebrate excellence and innovation in marketing.
